Blogs

I wrote different blogs about different topics of my interest. Sharing knowledge is important for me and it leaded me to create them.

  • Python: As the programming language used for most of my other blogs, I thought that it would be a good idea to introduce it. As one of the most popular programming language, having knowledge about it is always good.
  • Prometheus: I discovered this technology while working at OVHcloud. I found out that there was not that many blogs about it while it is very useful to collect metrics from servers. So I decided to write about it.
  • Artificial intelligence: This blog is about what I discovered during my master degree and used later in different professional projects. It is one of the new hot topics and I thought that curious persons would enjoy reading some articles about it.
  • Data Visualization: This topic is complementary of the previous one. How to show your data efficiently? How to represent the reality to its closest? One wrong step and the data could be misinterpreted, leading to huge cost for the company when making important choices.
  • Recommendation system: I followed a class at university about it and I found it intriging. Then in my different jobs, I noticed the importance of information and how long it can be to find what you need among long documentations. A good recommendatioon system for documentation can be life changing in your professional life. Those reasons pushed me to write about this topic.
