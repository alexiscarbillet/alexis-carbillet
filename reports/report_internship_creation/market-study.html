Chapter 3 Market study

This market study as been divided in two parts. The first one is the analysis of the competition and the other one is a sidewalk.

In the first part, analysis of the competition has been done. It used an Excel file. For each rows, a company was analyzed. Each column corresponds to a research topic. Some topics were our advantage compare to them, their principal functions and what are their main assets for the customer. At the end of the study, 63 competitors have been studied during the analysis.

Then a sidewalk has been prepared. It took place in Marseille. In 3 days, we had 50 answers to our survey from companies, 100 answers from people on the sidewalk and 100 others from a google form send to students from the main cities of France. There were 3 questionnaires, one for each survey. For examples, some questions asked were:

  • What is your sector of activity?

  • How much do you tolerate advertising on an application?

The questionnaire from the google form is very similar to the one for the sidewalk. The only difference was a question in order to know where they are from.

Knowing that people are interested by the project, the next stage was to begin the advertisement in order to attract new users.
